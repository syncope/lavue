Live Image Viewer¶

LaVue is a simple implementation of a live viewer front end. It is supposed to show a live image view from xray-detectors at PETRA3 @ desy.de, e.g. Pilatus, Lambda, Eiger, PerkinElmer, PCO, LimaCCD, and others.

Authors: Christoph Rosemann <christoph.rosemann at desy.de>, Jan Kotański <jan.kotanski at desy.de>, André Rothkirch <andre.rothkirch at desy.de>
